PRIVACY

How this site handles your data

Last updated {{ lastUpdated }}

The short version

This site records anonymous analytics so I can see which projects resonate. No names, no emails, no ad networks, no third-party trackers, no selling of anything to anyone. If that's all you wanted to know, you can stop reading here.

What gets collected

When you visit a page, the following is recorded:

  • The page you viewed and how long you stayed on it
  • How you arrived (referrer hostname only — e.g. linkedin.com, not the full URL)
  • Your browser and whether you're using a touch device or a mouse
  • Project card clicks and outbound link clicks (so I can see what people open)
  • A random identifier (UUID) stored in your browser, so I can tell that three page views from the same visit are actually the same visit

What does not get collected

  • Your name, email, IP address, or any contact information
  • Form fields, keystrokes, or mouse movements
  • Third-party advertising or tracking pixels
  • Anything Google Analytics, Meta, or similar would collect — there are no such scripts on this site

Where it's stored

Events are written to Supabase, a managed Postgres database. Row-level security restricts read access to my authenticated session only. The visitor identifier lives in your browser's localStorage and is rotated every 30 days, so the same person appears as a "new visitor" at least once a month.

How long it's kept

Event records are kept indefinitely so I can compare year-over-year trends. Because no record is tied to a real person, "deleting your data" essentially means rotating your browser's UUID — which you can do at any time by clearing site data in your browser settings, or by waiting up to 30 days for the automatic rotation.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have the right to know what's being collected and to request its deletion. Since no record is linked to your identity, the practical fulfillment of either request is the same: clear your browser's site data for amandarae.dev, and the link between you and any prior event is permanently severed.

If you'd like written confirmation, email {{ contactEmail }}.

Changes to this policy

If I change what gets collected, this page gets updated and the "Last updated" date at the top changes. Material changes will also be called out in a banner on the home page for at least 30 days.
